选型指南

What to Send Before We Recommend a 系列

The fastest recommendation usually starts from interface geometry, media, pressure range, and how the connector will actually be used on the line。

1. 接口几何尺寸

螺纹类型、管外径、密封面、接口深度和可用安装空间都会影响正确结构。

2. 介质与压力

请说明管路介质是空气、氮气、氦气、水、冷却液、油还是混合工艺介质，并提供压力范围。

3. 使用节奏

Cycle count, takt time, and whether the connection is manual or automated strongly influence the recommended actuation logic。

4. 材料选择

Seal and body materials affect temperature range, cleanliness, media compatibility, and long-term sealing stability。
