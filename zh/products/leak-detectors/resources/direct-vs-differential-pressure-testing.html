直压 vs 差压 气密检漏

直压 and differential pressure testing both evaluate pressure change over time, but they fit different leak limits, part volumes, and production requirements。

资源概览

项目前期评估实用说明

This resource is adapted from leak testing training material and rewritten for international website use. It is intended as a selection guide, not a final test specification。

直压测试

直压压降 measures pressure loss in the test part or test volume after the part is charged and isolated. It is usually easier to understand, easier to set up, and suitable for straightforward air-tightness checks。

差压测试

差压 compares the test side against a reference side. It can improve sensitivity for small pressure differences, but the fixture, reference volume, temperature behavior, and stabilization need more careful control。

如何选择

Start with leak target, test pressure, part volume, material flexibility, required cycle time, and whether the station needs electronic pressure regulation. L5 models are used for differential pressure, while L3 models are used for direct pressure。
