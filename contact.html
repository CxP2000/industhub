FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What should we send in the first inquiry?

The fastest route is to send the interface geometry, thread or tube size, pressure or vacuum range, media type, cycle requirement, and any leak-rate target. A drawing or part photo helps even more.

Can you help with liquid cooling connector selection?

Yes. We can help review coolant media, loop layout, service frequency, spill sensitivity, and available installation space to narrow the right quick connector concept.

Do you offer custom connector designs?

Yes. When a standard connector does not match the part geometry or process requirement, we can review a custom sealing head or connection concept based on your drawings and conditions.

Can you help choose the leak detection method too?

Yes. If you share your test pressure, part volume, acceptable leak rate, and cycle-time limit, we can help compare pressure decay, differential pressure, mass flow, or helium methods.

Can we request samples or a recommendation first?

Yes. Many projects begin with a recommendation, a drawing review, or evaluation samples before moving into larger-volume purchasing.
