Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a contact form, request a quote, or communicate with IndustHub via our website or email, we may collect: your name, company name, email address, phone number, and details about your application or inquiry. We also automatically collect standard server log information including IP address, browser type, and pages visited for security and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to respond to your inquiries, provide requested technical consultations, process quote requests, and improve our website experience. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share data with authorized distributors or technical partners solely as needed to fulfill your specific request.

3. Cookies & Tracking

This website uses only essential session cookies and anonymous analytics cookies to understand site usage and improve performance. No advertising or behavioral targeting cookies are deployed. You may configure your browser to reject cookies, though this may affect some site functionality.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL), access controls, and regular security reviews.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by applicable law. Inquiries and correspondence are typically retained for up to 3 years unless an ongoing business relationship exists.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including GDPR for EU residents), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data, as well as the right to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your data rights, contact us at privacy@industhub.com or through our Contact page.
