Why This Method

What Decides the Right Leak Test Route

The right model is usually decided by leak target, test pressure, part volume, cycle-time allowance, and fixture repeatability.

Sensitivity Target

Differential pressure is usually preferred when small pressure changes need better resolution.

Pressure Regulation

Electronic regulation helps repeatability and recipe switching; manual regulation keeps setup simple.

Fixture Interface

Sealing points, part support, and quick connector route directly affect the final test result.

Production Integration

Data output, pass/fail logic, PLC signals, and operator workflow can be reviewed by project.
