Selection Logic

What Helps Us Point You to the Right Page

A short application description is usually enough to choose the first route. More detailed model selection happens after the product family is clear.

1. Interface or Part Geometry

Thread standard, tube OD, port depth, sealing face, coolant port, or fixture photo helps route connector inquiries quickly.

2. Test Method or Quality Target

Leak target, test pressure, part volume, cycle time, and regulation style help choose L3 or L5 leak tester routes.

3. Process and Operator Workflow

Filling, evacuation, pressure testing, liquid cooling service, manual handling, or automation changes the recommended route.

4. Custom or Future Equipment Needs

If the request does not fit current connector or leak tester families, it can start as engineering support or a future product route.
