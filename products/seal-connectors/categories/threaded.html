Selection Guide

Information Needed for Threaded Connector Selection

Thread direction, thread standard, engagement height, pressure, media, and operation mode decide the correct structure.

1. Internal or External Thread

Confirm whether the connector seals onto a male thread or into a female threaded port.

2. Thread Size and Height

Send thread standard, diameter, pitch, thread height, and available sealing face.

3. Pressure and Media

Pressure class and media compatibility determine body material and seal material.

4. Manual or Automated Use

Bench testing, manual stations, and automated fixtures often require different actuation styles.

Have a Threaded Port to Seal?

Send the thread size, pressure, media, operation mode, and a photo or drawing so we can recommend the right series.
